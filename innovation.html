Innovation - Artifacts of History

Age 1

Ark of the Covenant

  1. Return a card from your hand. Transfer all cards of the same color from the boards of all players with no top Artifacts to your score pile. If Ark of the Covenant is a top card on any board, transfer it to your hand.

Basur Hoyuk Tokens

  1. Draw and reveal a 4. If you have a top card of the drawn card's color that comes before it in the alphabet, return the drawn card and all cards from your score pile.

Lurgan Canoe

  1. Meld a card from your hand. Score all other cards of the same color from your board. If you scored at least one card, repeat this effect.

Papyrus of Ani

  1. Return a purple card from your hand. If you do, draw and reveal a card of any type of value two higher. If the drawn card is purple, meld it and execute each of its non-demand effects. Do not share them.

Priest-King

  1. Score a card from your hand. If you have a top card matching its color, execute each of the top card's non-demand dogma effects. Do not share them.
  2. Claim an achievement, if eligible.

Pavlovian Tusk

  1. Draw three cards of value equal to your top green card. Return one, score one, keep one.

Electrum Stater of Efesos
