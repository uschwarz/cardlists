Innovation - Artifacts of History

Age 1

Ark of the Covenant

  1. Return a card from your hand. Transfer all cards of the same color from the boards of all players with no top Artifacts to your score pile. If Ark of the Covenant is a top card on any board, transfer it to your hand.

Basur Hoyuk Tokens

  1. Draw and reveal a 4. If you have a top card of the drawn card's color that comes before it in the alphabet, return the drawn card and all cards from your score pile.

Lurgan Canoe

  1. Meld a card from your hand. Score all other cards of the same color from your board. If you scored at least one card, repeat this effect.

Papyrus of Ani

  1. Return a purple card from your hand. If you do, draw and reveal a card of any type of value two higher. If the drawn card is purple, meld it and execute each of its non-demand effects. Do not share them.

Jiskairumoko Necklace

  1. you to return a card from your score pile! If you do, transfer an achievement of the same value from your achievements to mine!

Treaty of Kadesh

  1. you to return all top cards with a demand effect from your board!
  2. Score a top, non-blue card with a demand effect from your board.

Priest-King

  1. Score a card from your hand. If you have a top card matching its color, execute each of the top card's non-demand dogma effects. Do not share them.
  2. Claim an achievement, if eligible.

Pavlovian Tusk

  1. Draw three cards of value equal to your top green card. Return one, score one, keep one.

Dancing Girl

  1. you to transfer Dancing Girl to your board!
  2. If Dancing Girl has been on every board during this action, and it started on your board, you win.

Tale of the Shipwrecked Sailor

  1. Choose a color. Draw a 1. Meld a card of the chosen color from your hand. If you do, splay that color left.

Holemgard Bows

  1. you to transfer the highest top card with a CASTLE on your board to my hand!
  2. Draw a 2.

Xianrendong Shards

  1. Reveal three cards from your hand. Score two, then tuck the other. If the scored cards were the same color, draw three 1s.

Electrum Stater of Efesos

  1. Draw and reveal a 3. If you do not have a top card of the drawn card's color, meld it and repeat this effect.

Sibidu Needle

  1. Draw and reveal a 1. If you have a top card of matching color and value to the drawn card, score the drawn card and repeat this effect.

Mask of Warka

  1. Choose a color. Each player reveals all cards of that color from their hand. If you are the only player to reveal cards, return them and claim all achievements of value matching those cards, ignoring eligibility.

Age 9

Luna 3

  1. Return all cards from your score pile. Draw and score a card of value equal to the number of cards returned.

Philips Compact Cassette

  1. you to unsplay all splayed colors on your board!
  2. Splay up to two colors on your board.

United Nations Charter

  1. you to transfer all top cards with demand effects on your board to my score pile!
  2. If you have a top card on your board with a demand effect, draw a 10.

Syncom 3

  1. Return all cards from your hand. Draw and reveal five 9. If you revealed all five colors, you win.

Rock Around the Clock

  1. For each top card on your board with a [Clock], draw and score a 9.

Magnavox Odyssey

  1. Draw and meld two 10. If they are the same color, you win.

The Big Bang

  1. Execute the non-demand effects of your top blue card, without sharing. If this caused any change to occur, draw and remove a 10 from the game, then repeat this effect.

Yaeger's Bell X-1A

  1. Draw and meld a 9. Execute the effects on the melded card as if they were on this card, without sharing. If that card has [Clock], repeat this effect.

Marylin Diptych

  1. You may score a card from your hand. You may transfer any card from your score pile to your hand. If you have exactly 25 points, you win.

Velcro Shoes

  1. you to transfer a 9 from your hand to my hand! If you do not, transfer a 9 from your score pile to my score pile! If you do neither, I win!

Age 10

Maastricht Treaty

  1. If you have the most cards in your score pile, you win.

Where's Waldo?

  1. You win.

Twister

  1. you to reveal your score pile! For each color, meld a card of that color from your score pile!

Maldives

  1. you to return all cards i your hand but two! Return all cards in your score pile but two!
  2. Return all cards in your score pile but four.

Seikan Tunnel

  1. If you have the most cards of a color showing on your board out of all colors on all boards, you win.

Rover Curiosity

  1. Draw and meld an Artifact 10. Execute the effects of the melded card as if they were on this card. Do not share them.

Higgs Boson

  1. Transfer all cards on your board to your score pile.

Exxon Valdez

  1. you to remove all cards from your hand, score pile, board, and achievements from the game! You lose! If there is only one player remaining in the game, that player wins!

Dolly the Sheep

  1. You may score your bottom yellow card. You may tuck a 1. If your bottom yellow card is Domestication, you win. Otherwise, meld the highest card in your hand, then draw a 10.

DeLorean DMC-12

  1. If DeLorean DMC-12 is a top card on any board, remove all top cards on all boards and all cards in all hands from the game.
